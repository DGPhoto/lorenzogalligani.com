The Kaktus People project

This project delves into the lives of individuals residing in the Kaktus Towers in central Copenhagen. Despite living in a similar, unchangeable spaces, each home uniquely mirrors the personality of its occupant. Every morning, they wake up to almost identical apartments and views from their windows. Like the buildings, which appear so different depending on the light and the perspective, so too are the tenants, very different people who live very different lives. My goal is to capture the distinct personality of each tenant, showing the beauty in their individuality. The project began in November 2022 and is an ongoing exploration of the varied lives within the Kaktus Towers. .
